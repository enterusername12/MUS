    Posts & Community

    How do I share a post?▼

    To share a post, click on the share icon below any post. You can then choose to share it within the platform or copy the link to share externally.

    How do I view the full details of a post?▼

    Click on any post to view its full details, including all comments, attachments, and additional information.

    How do I like or comment on a post?▼

    Click the heart icon to like a post. To comment, click on the comment icon or the post itself, then type your comment in the text box.

    Polls & Voting

    How do I participate in polls?▼

    When you see a poll, simply click on your preferred option to cast your vote. Results are typically visible after voting.

    Calendar & Events

    How do I check upcoming events?▼

    Navigate to the Calendar section to view all upcoming events. You can filter by date, category, or event type.

    What do the different colored dots mean on the calendar?▼

    Colored dots indicate different event categories. Check the calendar legend for specific color meanings for your institution.

    Feedback

    How do I submit feedback?▼

    You can submit feedback through the "Submit Feedback" button or by contacting support directly with your suggestions.

    Rewards & Points

    How do I earn reward points?▼

    Earn reward points by actively participating in the community, attending events, completing challenges, and engaging with content.

    How do I redeem my reward points?▼

    Visit the Rewards section to view available rewards and redeem your points for merchandise, discounts, or special privileges.

    Account

    How do I edit my profile?▼

    Go to your account settings and click on "Edit Profile" to update your information, photo, and preferences.

    Merchandise

    How do I browse and purchase merchandise?▼

    Visit the Merchandise section to browse available items. Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.

    Campus News

    How do I stay updated with campus news?▼

    Check the Campus News section regularly for the latest updates, announcements, and stories from your campus community.
